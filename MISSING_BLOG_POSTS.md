4. **H2 Sections:** 3-5 main points
5. **H3 Subsections:** Details, examples, tips
6. **Lists:** Bullet points for scanability
7. **Tool callouts:** 2-3 internal links per post to tool pages

10. **Metadata:** SEO title (60 chars), meta description (160 chars)
